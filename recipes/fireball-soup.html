Spicy Fireball Soup

Adequately spiced fireball soup

Get ready to spew fire from all orifices with this tantalising recipe from Chef Cook

  • 8 gallons of premium fuel
  • 1 Large siphon
  • Cigarette lighter or a box of matches
  • (Optional, a bottle of injecter cleaner or octane boost for extra spicyness)
  1. Using siphon pour out desired amount of fuel for meal size into a cup
  2. Place cup on to a flat surface whilst we perpare the other ingredients leaving enough room to add a dash of any extras
  3. Either ready the lighter in your dominant hand or strike a match ready
  4. Swallow contents of cup
  5. stick out your tounge and GENTLY light the tip, taking care not to burn your nose
  6. And bon appetit!
